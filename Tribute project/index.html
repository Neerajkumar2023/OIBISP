Biography

Mahendra Singh Dhoni is one of the most well known cricketers in the world. Successful cricketers in Indian history. He hold several records, including mmost runs scored in ICC tournaments, most stumping done by him and the fastest half-century in ODIs.He is also the first captain who win all three ICC trophies, namely the T-20 World Cup in 2007, World Cup in 2011 and Champions Trophy in 2013. He also received Padma Shri.
